CHEMISES

Intelligent Molecular Prediction Platform

What is Chemises?

The Chemises project presents an intelligent QSAR (Quantitative Structure–Activity Relationship) based system designed to predict chemical behavior and potential outcomes of molecular combinations using computational modeling.

Why Computational Chemistry?

Traditional laboratory experimentation in chemistry and pharmaceuticals demands significant time, cost, and resources. Computational approaches offer faster, scalable, and cost-effective alternatives for chemical analysis and drug discovery.

Powered by Molecular Data

Chemises leverages molecular data sourced from the PubChem API and extracts key descriptors such as: Molecular Weight, LogP, Hydrogen Bond Donors, Hydrogen Bond Acceptors, and more.
